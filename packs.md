# Packs & Rarities

Each pack contains a mix of cards across rarity tiers.

| Pack Type | Cost | Cards | Rare Chance |
|------------|------|--------|--------------|
| Bronze | 500 | 3 | 5% |
| Silver | 1,000 | 3 | 15% |
| Gold | 5,000 | 5 | 35% |

Every opening uses a pre-generated `SpinPlan` for deterministic results.
